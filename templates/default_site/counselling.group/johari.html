The Johari Window 'panes' can be changed in size to reflect the relevant proportions of each type of 'knowledge' of/about a particular person in a given group or team situation. The panes above are adjustable by hovering your mouse over the intersection of the panes. You can add text to the panes by clicking in them.

In new groups or teams the open free space for any team member is small because shared awareness is relatively small. 

As the team member becomes better established and known, so the size of the team member's open free area quadrant increases.
