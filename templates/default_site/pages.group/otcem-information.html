THE OCCUPATIONAL THERAPY COMPETENCY EVIDENCING MATRIX (OTCEM):
Self & Practice Educator Assessment Tool of Competence

The OTCEM competency statements are mapped to the Australian Minimum Competency Standards for New Graduate Occupational Therapists (ACSOT) Occupational Therapy Australia, 2010.

They have been mapped and directly linked within the website to the Performance Record for the Australian Competency Standards for Occupational Therapists (PRACSOT) Occupational Therapy Australia, 2010.

Once your competency evidenced within the OTCA Website OTCEM is verified by your practice educator and/or lecturer, they will be automatically be tick-off against the relevant PRACSOT performance criteria at the appropriate Competency Development Level: Emerging, Consolidating and Competent to Graduate.

For the purpose of this competency website, and with the permission of OTA, the PRACSOT 'Demonstrated Competence' categories have been amended to reflect the 3 Competency Development Levels.
