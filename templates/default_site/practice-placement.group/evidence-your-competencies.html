Self-Assess Your OTCEM

Here you self-assess your competency by ticking the OTCEM competency statements have achieved. This will save to your ePortfolio.

Your Practice Educator will verify your self-assessment by agreeing or disagreeing. This is Observable Evidence.

Click here for IMPORTANT INSTRUCTIONS & INFORMATION for Self-Assessing Your OTCEM . You MUST read this information first.

Your self-assessed OTCEM competency statements are displayed below, statements highlighted in yellow have been verified by your Practice Educator.

Purple indicates previously verified self-asssessments, whilst blue indicates un-verified self-assessments that are awaiting verification by your Practice Educator.

When you tick the competency statements that you have achieved for each step that you have focused on below, a box will appear on the left side of the page. Use this to save and upload your OTCEM self-assessment and any supporting hard evidence/description to your ePortfolio.

Then get your Practice Educator to verify your self-assessment.

You can also use the Save window to upload Documented Evidence, this is either a description or a piece of hard/documented evidence that supports your ticked self-assessment.

You DO NOT have to add an individual piece of Documented Evidence (description or hard evidence) for EACH ticked competency statement. Consult the Course Guide/Outline for your University Occupational Therapy Course for details on how much evidence is required. Check the Important Instruction Information link in the box above for further information about this.

See further information about how to self-assess and evidence your competence using the OTCEM in the Important Instruction Information link box above.
